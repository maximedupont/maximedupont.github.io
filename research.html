My research interests have been centered around physics so far, in particular in the area of condensed matter theory, with a focus on strongly interacting quantum systems. This branch of physics aims at understanding the properties of quantum matter. Especially, I have worked on quantum phases and transitions, dynamics and transport in quantum systems as well as disorder in quantum many-body systems. I have also closely collaborated with people performing experiments on quantum materials, against which theoretical models and predictions could be checked.

Keywords
  • quantum physics
  • condensed matter theory
  • strongly correlated systems
  • numerical simulations
  • quantum matter
  • quantum dynamics
  • disordered systems
  • experimental collaborations

To address such stimulating problems, I take advantage of large-scale, high-performance numerical simulations using Python and C++. This includes,

  • Working with linear algebra based techniques,
  • Developing Monte Carlo methods,
  • Solving challenging optimization problems,
  • Performing statistical analysis over large amount of datasets to extract relevant information and make predictions.
