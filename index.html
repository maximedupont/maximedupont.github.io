I develop algorithms that rely on large-scale numerical simulations to tackle physics problems and get a better understanding of the properties of quantum matter. Take a look at my publication list to find out about my research. :-)

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and University of California, Berkeley in Joel Moore's group.

Until September 2018, I was at Laboratoire de Physique Théorique in Toulouse, France, where I obtained my PhD in July 2018 under the supervision of Sylvain Capponi and Nicolas Laflorencie.
