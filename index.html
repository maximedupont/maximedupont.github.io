Hi! I am Maxime Dupont, a Research Scientist with a Ph.D. in theoretical physics.

My research seeks to understand how the collective behavior of many interacting particles governed by the laws of quantum mechanics leads to remarkable emergent phenomena. Find out more!

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and University of California, Berkeley in the group of Joel E. Moore.

During my Ph.D., I visited for three months in spring 2017 the Department of Physics at Boston University, where I worked in the group of Anders W. Sandvik.

Until September 2018, I was at the French National Centre for Scientific Research and the University of Toulouse in France, where I obtained my Ph.D. in July 2018 under the supervision of Sylvain Capponi and Nicolas Laflorencie.
