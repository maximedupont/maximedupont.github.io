Hi! I am Maxime Dupont, a Research Scientist with a Ph.D. in theoretical physics. I work on developing algorithms that rely on large-scale numerical simulations to tackle physics problems and get a better understanding of the properties of quantum matter. Find out more about my research! :-)

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and University of California, Berkeley in Joel Moore's group.

Until September 2018, I was at Laboratoire de Physique Théorique in Toulouse, France, where I obtained my Ph.D. in July 2018 under the supervision of Sylvain Capponi and Nicolas Laflorencie. My Ph.D. thesis entitled “Dynamics and disorder in quantum antiferromagnets” is available online.
